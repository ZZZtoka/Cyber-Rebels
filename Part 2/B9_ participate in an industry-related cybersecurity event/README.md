## Analysis
This event demonstrated how cybersecurity principles are applied in the financial industry to combat fraud and cybercrime. Modern fraud detection systems combine machine learning, behavioural analysis, risk scoring, and human analyst review to identify suspicious activities. The presentation also highlighted the increasing role of AI in both cyberattacks and cybersecurity defence systems. Attackers are now using AI-generated phishing emails, deepfakes, and automated scam campaigns, while defenders use AI to detect anomalies and improve fraud prevention. This reflects the growing importance of cybersecurity knowledge in financial systems and real-world organisations.

## Reflection
Attending this cybersecurity event improved my understanding of how financial institutions detect and respond to cyber threats. I learned how phishing attacks can lead to account takeovers and how AI technologies are changing both cyberattacks and cyber defence strategies. The event also gave me insight into potential cybersecurity career paths such as fraud analyst, SOC analyst, and threat intelligence analyst.
