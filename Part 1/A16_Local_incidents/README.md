## A16_3_Local_Security_Incidents

## Description
I explored common local security incidents to understand everyday threats affecting individuals and communities.

## Findings
- Theft incidents such as stolen bicycles or personal belongings on campus
- Scam or phishing messages targeting individuals through SMS or email
- Unauthorised access to buildings or restricted areas

## Analysis
Local security incidents show that everyday environments are still vulnerable to threats. Theft often occurs due to lack of supervision or weak physical security. Scam and phishing attacks target individuals by exploiting trust and lack of awareness. Unauthorised access can occur when access control measures are not properly enforced. These incidents highlight the importance of both physical and digital security, as well as user awareness in preventing such threats.

## Reflection
This activity helped me understand that security threats are not limited to large organisations but also occur in local environments. It emphasised the importance of staying alert and taking precautions in daily life.
